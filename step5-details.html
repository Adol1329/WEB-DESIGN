Step 5: Processing

Once all documents are submitted and fees are paid, your application will be processed. This may take some time, depending on:

  • Volume of applications
  • Completeness of submitted documents
  • Payment processing time

You will receive confirmation once the transfer is complete, and your new registration details will be sent to you.
