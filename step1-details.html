Step 1: Gather Documents

In this step, you need to gather all the required documents for the vehicle transfer. This typically includes:

  • Proof of ownership (e.g., the current vehicle title)
  • Identification (e.g., driver's license or passport)
  • Proof of address (e.g., utility bill or bank statement)

Make sure all documents are current and accurate to avoid delays in the transfer process.
