Transfer Process

Step-by-Step Guide

  • Step 1: Gather Documents

    Gather required documents, including proof of ownership and identification.

  • Step 2: Complete Application

    Complete the transfer application form and provide all necessary details.

  • Step 3: Submit Documents

    Submit the form and documents to our office or via our online portal.

  • Step 4: Pay Fees

    Pay any applicable fees. You will receive a confirmation of the transfer.

  • Step 5: Processing

    Wait for the processing period. Once completed, you will receive the new registration details.
